/ 01 — WHY

For me, first.

I am (still) learning Japanese. The tools I tried didn't fit the way I wanted to study, so I built my own. It turned into a proper little desktop app — image-based character display, pronunciation info, stroke counts, and a JSON-driven bulk import for when I wanted to add hundreds of kanji at once.

And because I wanted it to feel like a real Windows program, I created the MSI installer.
